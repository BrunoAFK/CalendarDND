<en-US>
Calendar DND silences your phone automatically during calendar meetings and restores sound when they end. It’s reliable on Samsung devices, respects your manual DND changes, and runs efficiently in the background.

Key features:
  - Automatic DND based on calendar events
  - Smart merging for back‑to‑back meetings
  - Respects manual DND changes during meetings
  - Personalized filters: calendars, busy‑only, ignore all‑day, minimum duration, skip recurring, days of week, location required, title keywords (whole word, starts/ends with, exact, regex)
  - Timing offsets and pre‑meeting warnings
  - Post‑meeting notification with one‑tap extend option
  - One‑time actions on the next event (enable or skip)
  - Vibration cool‑down after DND ends (vibrate‑only)
  - Theme selector + legacy theme option
  - Multiple languages

Privacy:
  - All calendar data stays on your device
  - No ads

Prefer no Firebase? Get the non‑Firebase build from GitHub:
https://github.com/BrunoAFK/CalendarDND

Note: Any data collection is limited to crash reports, performance monitoring, and improvement metrics. You can opt out in Privacy settings.
