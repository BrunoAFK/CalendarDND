Why Calendar DND?

Instant & Automatic

Set it once, forget forever. DND activates the moment your meeting starts and turns off when it ends.

Smart Meeting Merging

Back-to-back meetings? No problem. Calendar DND keeps you silent through consecutive meetings without gaps.

Respects Your Choices

Manually changed DND during a meeting? The app notices and backs off. You're always in control.

Privacy First

All processing happens on your device. Your calendar data never leaves your phone. No accounts needed.

Battery Friendly

Efficient scheduling with minimal background activity. Your battery won't notice it's running.

Highly Configurable

Filter by calendar, busy status, event duration, all-day events, or keywords. Make it work exactly how you want.

How It Works

1

Install & Enable

Download the app and flip the automation switch on.

2

Grant Permissions

Allow calendar access and DND control. The app guides you through it.

3

Forget About It

That's it. Your phone now silences itself during meetings automatically.

Requirements

  • Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher
  • A calendar app with synced events
  • Works great on Samsung devices
